Directions:
1. Enter your information in the settings page.
If you want to record where you are drinking, turn location settings on! Add your favorite drink to the drinks list!
2. When you are ready to start drinking, select "Start My Night"

You will be brought to your Night homepage.
4. As you consume drinks select their category and specifics and hit Drink!

5. Throughout the night you can check what your BAC is, and other information in Stats.

6. Once you are done drinking for the night, press "End my night" and you will be sent back to iDranks homepage where you can view your saved nights in "Past Nights"
